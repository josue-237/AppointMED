Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is AppointMED?

    AppointMED is a web application that allows you to schedule appointments with your doctor. You can also view your appointments and see your doctor's availability.


  • How do I schedule an appointment?

    To schedule an appointment, you don't need to sign up. Just schedule an appointment by clicking on the "Home" tab on the navigation bar. You can then select the doctor you would like to schedule an appointment with and the date and time you would like to schedule the appointment.


  • How do I view my appointments?

    Since AppointMED doesn't use accounts you have to save the url or qrcode you are given after making an appointment.


  • How do I view my doctor's availability?

    Clik on the "Home" tab and choose a doctor. After that you can select a date you would like to view availability for.
